Set Clear Goals

Break your study sessions into manageable tasks. Use the SMART framework (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) to plan effectively.

Take Regular Breaks

Utilize techniques like the Pomodoro Method to maintain focus. Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat for sustained productivity.

Eliminate Distractions

Turn off unnecessary notifications and create a quiet environment. Apps like Forest help you stay focused by limiting phone use.

Utilize Past Papers

Review previous exams and assignments to familiarize yourself with patterns and expectations. Practicing with old tests can boost confidence.
